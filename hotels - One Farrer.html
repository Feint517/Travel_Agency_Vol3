The iconic beacon in the Farrer Park district, One Farrer Hotel is located in the middle of a rich heritage and cultural enclave, set amidst new and dynamic developments of the future. A luxury retreat designed for the discerning traveller, this five-star urban resort offers an intuitive approach towards lifestyle and hospitality, with unique and distinct accommodation, F&B, conference and spa offerings. One Farrer Hotel offers 237 well-appointed rooms within a 'Hotels within a Hotel' concept, comprising of three distinct hotels - Urban Hotel, Loft Apartments and Skyline Hotel & Sky Villas.
