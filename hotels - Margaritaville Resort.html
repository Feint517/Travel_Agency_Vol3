A tropical oasis in New York City's world-famous Times Square, Margaritaville Resort Times Square invites you to set your watch to island time. Whether you're kicking back in your breezy room or suite, lounging poolside on the rooftop deck with a frozen concoction in hand, or taking a culinary tour through the Caribbean at our restaurants, this relaxing retreat is your passport to paradise. And when you're ready for adventure, you'll find many of the city's most famous attractions within walking distance, including the theaters of Broadway, Central Park and the iconic Empire State Building.
