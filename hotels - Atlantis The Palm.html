Atlantis, The Palm is a 1,544-room ocean-themed destination resort. Spectacularly situated on the crescent of Dubai's iconic Palm Island. With views across Dubai’s iconic skyline and Arabian sea, this majestic resort offers unique rooms and unforgettable experiences for everyone. From the moment you arrive, you will be immersed in new experiences that you could have never even imagined. Thrill-seekers can enjoy complimentary access to Aquaventure Waterpark, the world's largest waterpark, during their stay. Also voted as TripAdvisor’s No.1 waterpark with over 105 attractions. Guests can make the most of Dubai's endless sunshine and spend their days by the pool or relaxing on a pristine private beach.
